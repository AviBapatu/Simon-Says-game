How to Play Simon Says
1. Getting Started

Press any key to begin the game. Watch and remember the sequence!

2. Game Pattern

Simon will show you a sequence of colored buttons. Each level adds one more step to remember.

3. Your Turn

Repeat the sequence by clicking the buttons in the same order.

4. Scoring

Each successful sequence increases your level. Try to beat your high score!
